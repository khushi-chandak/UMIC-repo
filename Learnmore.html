About Us

UMIC at IIT Bombay pioneers unmanned aerial and ground vehicle advancements. Established in the mid-2000s, it birthed STAB (Institute Technical Council), fostering technical enthusiasm. With 80+ undergrads from diverse backgrounds, the team aims for cutting-edge autonomous solutions.

Our Vision

Team SeDriCa endeavours to pioneer India's first Level 5 Autonomous car, designed to navigate Indian road conditions. With a focus on efficiency and safety, we aim to excel in national and international competitions, notably the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition (IGVC) at Oakland University, Michigan.
